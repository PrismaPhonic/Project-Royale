About Us

ROYALE is the new project from label head Peter Farr and singer Annie Jacobsen who released their groundbreaking future garage single “Eternal” on Chillage Records. Chillage’s label head, Peter Farr's alias Urple Eeple’s latest offering, “Lost in Motion” EP received critical acclaim from Mixmag, Nest HQ, The 405, Purple Sneakers and YourEDM and included groundbreaking remixes from Pixelord, Sweatson Klank, and Conrad Clifton.

As Seapora, Jacobsen’s last album “Seapop” featured work by a slew of talented producers, including Spoken Bird and Elevated Mind. She worked early on in her career with Alex Blue and Jay Wiltzen who would later go on to form the dynamic electronic duo BLU J, known well for their official ODESZA remix
